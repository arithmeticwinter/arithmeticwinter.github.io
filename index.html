Some Analogs of the Riemann-Roch Theorem

Selçuk Demir - Özyeğin University

Abstract: We will cover some analogs of the Riemann-Roch theorem.

Algebraic Function Fields

Haydar Göral - İzmir Institute of Technology - website & Nihan Tanısalı - INRIA-Saclay - website

Abstract: Function fields, algebraic extensions, valuations, valuation rings, places of function fields

Haydar Göral - İzmir Institute of Technology - website & Nihan Tanısalı - INRIA-Saclay - website

Algebraic Curves

Berkay Kebeci - Koç University - website

Abstract: Affine and projective space, algebraic curves, morphisms and rational functions, the vector space of divisors, the Riemann-Roch theorem for curves.
